Boond : Donate Blood Today!

Boond is being developed with the idea of efficiently integrating all the components involved under the operation of Blood Donation. It aims to bring together various stakeholders, such as potential blood donors, patients wishing to receive blood, blood banks and their inventories, hospitals and government officials overseeing the entire process. Our platform will facilitate multiple user login and profile maintenance for these stakeholders.
A real time dynamic inventory of various blood groups will be accessible to everyone logged onto the system. Blood banks will be required to update their inventory periodically which will be reflected to the users. Patients wishing to receive blood can request for reservation of blood on the system.
The system will then contact potential donors through personalized notifications in case of emergency blood requirement. This matchmaking will be done through profile mapping of donors and recipients, considering compatibility of blood types. In case of emergencies, recipients or their kin can search for nearby blood banks based upon their location.
To increase participation of people in blood donation, the system will also provide information about blood donation camps and events. Camp/event organizers will upload data about these events, and notifications will be sent accordingly to all users. FAQ sections will be provided, for common queries by stakeholders.
